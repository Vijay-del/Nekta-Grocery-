Safety First, Always
At Nekta, your life matters more than any delivery. Read these rules carefully before every shift.
⛑
Always Wear a Helmet
No helmet = no delivery. This is non-negotiable. A helmet protects your life every single ride.
🚫 MANDATORY
📱
No Phone While Riding
Never use your phone while the bike is moving. Pull over safely to check orders or messages.
🚫 MANDATORY
🌧
Slow Down in Rain
Wet roads are dangerous. Reduce speed by at least 30% during rain. Your safety beats speed.
🚨 HIGH PRIORITY
🚥
Follow All Traffic Signals
Red means stop – always. No order is worth risking your life at a signal.
🛣
Use the Left Lane
Always ride in the left lane. Do not overtake from the left on highways or main roads.
🌙
Night Riding Caution
Keep headlights on. Wear reflective gear if possible. Reduce speed at night by 20%.
🔧
Check Your Bike Daily
Tyres, brakes, lights – check before every shift. A 2-minute check can save your life.
😴
Don't Ride When Tired
Fatigue is as dangerous as drunk driving. If you're tired, rest. Orders can wait, you can't.

📄 Rider Agreement
By using the Nekta Rider app, you agree to the following:

• You are an independent delivery partner, not a Nekta employee.
• You are responsible for your own vehicle, fuel, and insurance.
• You must have a valid driving license and bike documents at all times.
• Earnings are based on completed deliveries only – cancelled orders earn ₹0.
• Nekta may deactivate accounts for misconduct, customer complaints, or safety violations.
• Cash collected from customers must be handed to the store owner promptly.
• Never misuse customer data – phone numbers are for delivery only.
⭐ Incentive Terms
• 15-order bonus (₹300) is credited within 24 hours of qualifying.
• Rain incentive (+₹10/order) is activated by admin only during heavy rain.
• Extra hours bonus (₹200 for 8h online) is tracked automatically by the app.
• Incentives are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash directly.
• Nekta reserves the right to modify incentive terms with 24h notice.
